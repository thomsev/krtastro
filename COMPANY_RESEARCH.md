# Karmøy Rørteknikk — public research notes

Last reviewed: 22 June 2026

This file documents the public facts used in the website. It is not customer-facing copy. Facts should still be confirmed by the company before public launch, particularly current contacts, employee count, certifications, and project permissions.

## Verified or strongly supported facts

- Legal name: Karmøy Rørteknikk AS.
- Organization number: 993 234 532.
- Established in 2008.
- Address: Husøyvegen 264, 4262 Avaldsnes.
- Public main phone: 480 82 015.
- Approximately 30–32 employees in recent public registers; the website therefore says “30+”.
- Registered activity: plumbing/VVS work.
- The company describes itself as a technical general contractor involved throughout the building phase.
- Publicly described capabilities include installation, refurbishment, heating, cooling, district heating, heat pumps, energy carriers, compressed air, sprinklers, water balancing, FG sprinkler control, gas control, bathrooms, industrial piping, TIG welding, and plastic welding.
- Approved apprenticeship company for the plumbing trade, with active apprentices shown in the public education register.
- Public approval listings show:
  - design of sanitary installations, class 2;
  - design of heating and cooling installations, class 2;
  - execution of sanitary installations, class 3;
  - execution of fire-extinguishing installations, class 3;
  - execution of heating and cooling installations, class 3.

## Public project references found

The legacy company page lists projects including Giske School energy plant, FMC Biopolymer, Helse Fonna, Stord Dental Clinic, Mykje School, Skjold School, Stølen Elderly Centre, and several municipal energy installations. A current construction database also associates the business with Karmsund upper-secondary school workshops, Brannstasjonen Borettslag, public buildings, warehouses, schools, care facilities, and sports facilities.

Do not present generated imagery as photographs of these projects. Current generated assets are labelled “Illustrasjonsbilde” in the UI.
